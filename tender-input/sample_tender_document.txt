Tender Number: TSD/2025/001

Tender Title: Provision of Security Guarding Services for Municipal Buildings

Scope of Work:
The appointed service provider will be required to supply, manage and oversee
security personnel at various municipal buildings within the Johannesburg
metropolitan area.  Services must include access control, patrols, control room
operations, incident reporting and emergency response as detailed in Section
3 of the tender document.

Mandatory Returnable Documents:
1. Completed and signed Form of Offer and Acceptance.
2. Tax clearance certificate / pin.
3. BBBEE Certificate or sworn affidavit.
4. PSIRA Registration Certificate.
5. Proof of CIDB grading.

Evaluation Criteria:
Tenders will be evaluated on price and functionality.  The functionality
criteria include company experience (30%), methodology and approach (40%), and
capacity / resources (30%).  A minimum functionality score of 70% must be
achieved to proceed to price evaluation.

The pricing schedule must include all costs associated with the provision of
services, including supervision, uniforms, equipment and administrative
